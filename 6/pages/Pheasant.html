Pheasants (/\xcb\x88f\xc9\x9bz\xc9\x99nt/) are birds of several genera within the subfamily Phasianinae, of the family Phasianidae in the order Galliformes. The family\'s native range is restricted to Asia.Pheasants are characterised by strong sexual dimorphism, males being highly decorated with bright colors and adornments such as wattles. Males are usually larger than females and have longer tails. Males play no part in rearing the young. Pheasants typically eat seeds and some insects.The best-known is the common pheasant, which is widespread throughout the world, in introduced feral populations and in farm operations. Various other pheasant species are popular in aviaries, such as the golden pheasant (Chrysolophus pictus).
